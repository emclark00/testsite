Humanities Without Walls
========================

is a consortium of humanities centers at sixteen research universities throughout the US Midwest and beyond that aims to create new avenues for collaborative research, teaching, and the production of scholarship in the humanities, forging and sustaining areas of inquiry that cannot be created or maintained without cross-institutional cooperation. Established in 2014 with the generous support of the Mellon Foundation, HWW is a large-scale experiment in collaboration as a dynamic scholarly practice, based at the [Humanities Research Institute](https://hri.illinois.edu/) at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign.
